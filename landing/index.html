About Us

BWC Construction Sdn Bhd (BWC) was incorporated on 7th July 1997 with the sole purpose of involving in any civil projects especially in site clearance, road works and ancillary works. Subsequently, BWC Construction ventured into construction of roads, drainage, bridge and slope protection. These have become BWC Construction Sdn Bhd areas of expertise with more than 20 years of experience, skills and knowledge in the construction field.
